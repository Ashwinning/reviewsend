How It Works

1

Send your draft to draft@reviewsend.com 

⚠ DO NOT INCLUDE your intended recipients in the `To`, `CC`, or `BCC` fields
Doing so will send your draft to the final recipient
2

Click 'Add Reviewers'

In the autoreply received to your email
3

Assign reviewers and recipients

You can configure whether to automatically send the email from your account if it has been edited, or when it has been reviewed by a certain person, people, or all marked reviewers
4

The reviewers receive your draft in their email

They may edit your email, or approve it for sending
5

Sent!

The email is sent to your intended recipients
